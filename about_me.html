About Me
Professional
Hi, my name is Paul Cassell (Castle) and I am a web designer. I currently work full-time for DealerOn, a car dealership website provider. My current position as a designer for DealerOn requires me to wear multiple hats from a designer's perspective. My daily tasks require me to cooperate with our client support, compliance, and our implementations teams. I complete immediate requests for the client support team through troubleshooting any conflicts the customer may be experiencing from the front end portion of their websites. I update OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) files through our FTP as well as assist in the creation of them. Those OEMs include Ford, Toyota, Nissan, and Mazda to name a few. I create custom pages for the implementations team through content migration projects. I am asked to create custom pages for a new client by comparing the pages that were made by the competitor and recreate them using my design sense/coding skills as well as following the best practices for the company. I have also made icons to add to the company's asset library that act as the company's own version of Font Awesome icons. I've also made some custom banners for pages using my graphic design abilities and experience using programs such as Photoshop or Illustrator.
Personal
