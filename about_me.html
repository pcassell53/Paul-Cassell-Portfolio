About Me
Professional
Hi, my name is Paul Cassell (Castle). I am a freelance UX Designer and a Data Analyst professionally. I am a research focused working professional that has a passion for design. I currently work full-time for DealerOn, a car dealership website provider. My current position is research focused, but my freelance services combine those skills I have learned to design quality products. I graduated from West Virginia University in 2015 with a Bachelor of Arts. I worked on some internships and a job working the front desk at a hotel before I was hired at DealerOn. When I was hired, I was assigned to research and audit API products for a data feed provider. Once I completed the project, I was assigned to work for the Technology and Business Operations department (where I still currently work). I have a more detailed explanation of my projects and work over the past couple of years at DealerOn here.
While I was working on these projects during the day, I would teach myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript through courses online in my spare time. This led to my interest in web design. I spoke to a front-end developer about some personal projects I had been working on and he stressed that my interests seemed to be more design focused. I did some reading and found that I had more of a passion for research-based design because of my natural skill set. Since then I have been working on personal projects and some freelance work to improve my UX Design skills.

Personal
I have a high interest in design, but some of my other hobbies include watching too many movies, playing video games, long-distance running, hanging out with my cat, and playing guitar. I really enjoy spending time with friends (like everyone else), but I also like to have time to myself too. I also like to re-design logos of sports teams and some companies that I think could use an update on their branding. It is a little difficult to not let design creep into some of my daily hobbies since it is something that I am naturally interested in. Regardless, I think that my personality is quite easy going and team oriented. I have confidence in my ability to adapt and contribute positive outcomes to any UX/UI Design team.
