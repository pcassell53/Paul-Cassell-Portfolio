UX Case Study

After dedicating myself to honing my coding skills and crafting static web pages, I embarked on exploring user experience case studies. It's important to note that this project was undertaken independently, during my free time, while I was enrolled in the "User Experience Design Essentials - Adobe XD UI UX Design" course on Udemy. This comprehensive course not only covers the basics of the User Experience (UX) process but also teaches practical skills in utilizing Adobe XD as a versatile UI design and prototyping tool. The course consists of two main projects, one of which is this self-guided endeavor. Throughout the duration of the project, the course's provided resources and materials, including a project brief and a persona brief, offered invaluable guidance and support whenever necessary.
